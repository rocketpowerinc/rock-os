# Android Dashboard

This dashboard is the Android corner of Rock-OS.

Use it for phone and tablet setup notes, app lists, offline maps, backup
habits, local sync workflows, recovery steps, and anything else that helps a
mobile device stay useful when the wider internet is being dramatic.

## Good Uses

- Android setup and hardening notes.
- Offline app and APK inventory.
- Backup and restore checklists.
- Local file sync workflows.
- Messaging, maps, documents, and media notes for offline use.
- Recovery reminders for the small glass rectangle everyone suddenly depends on.

## Practical Android Notes

Phones are often the easiest local clients during an outage. They have screens,
batteries, cameras, radios, storage, and just enough chaos to keep life
interesting.

Keep Android notes focused on repeatable workflows:

- Which apps matter offline.
- Where backups live.
- How files sync to the local network.
- How to restore or rebuild the device without guessing.

## Dashboard Notes

Dashboards are public local content. They are not encrypted with `git-crypt`.
Put private notes under Profiles instead.
