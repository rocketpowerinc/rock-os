# Pirating Dashboard

This dashboard is a local command center for pirating-themed sea notes, ship
references, route ideas, supplies, weather resources, and anything else that
should be available from Rock-OS when the internet is being dramatic.

## Good Uses

- Pirating and sea-travel checklists.
- Boat maintenance and inspection reminders.
- Weather, tide, and navigation resource links.
- Gear inventories and provisioning notes.
- Emergency procedures worth finding before stress does its little tap dance.

## Dashboard Notes

Dashboards are public local content. They are not encrypted with `git-crypt`.
Put private notes under Profiles instead.
